FAQ

How to connect the watch? expand_more

1. Enable Wi-Fi debugging on your watch.
2. Enter the watch's IP address in the app.
3. Tap "Connect".

Does it support Samsung Galaxy Watch? expand_more

Yes, the app works on all watches with Wear OS 4.0 and higher (Samsung, Pixel Watch, TicWatch).
